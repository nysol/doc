
\section{$>$ : Greater than comparison operator\label{sect:gt}}
\subsection*{Format}
ZDD Object  $zdd1$ $>$ $zdd2$

\subsection*{Description}
Compare itemsets in $zdd1$ with itemsets in $zdd2$, select items where the weight is greater than $zdd2$.
