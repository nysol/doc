
\section{+ : Addition operator\label{sect:plus}}
\subsection*{Format}
$zdd1$ + $zdd2$ $\rightarrow$ $zdd3$

\subsection*{Description}
Addition of weights for the same itemsets corresponding to $zdd1$ and $zdd2$.
